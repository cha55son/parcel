Parcel Help

The depot is located in the center of town. Houses, factories, and offices may place orders to the depot for later delivery. If you hover over the depot you will have the option to ship all parcels in storage to their intended destinations.

Delivery locations can place orders to the depot by hovering over them and clicking the order button. Each delivery location has a specific address which the ordered parcel will be delivered too.

You can place additional delivery locations by Shift + Left Click anywhere in the city.
